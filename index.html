Publications

Centre for Nigerian Leadership Perspective, Cenlep are the publishers of the first ever Leadership Encyclopedia which captures the details of Nigerian Leaders accross the country down to the grassroot. The publication comprises of the Political Class and Royal Class Encyclopedia each published in six volumes according to the six geo-political zones of Nigeria. The Centre for Nigerian Leadership Perspective, Cenlep is currently in the process of updating this iconic publication for research purposes and posterity documentation.

Political Class Encyclopedia

The Political Class Encyclopedia captures the details of all elected Government officials in Nigeria ranging from Mr. President to the various Ward Councillors.

Royal Class Encyclopedia

The Royal Class Encyclopedia captures the details of all Traditional Rulers across the country ranging from the First Class Chiefs to the Village Heads.
